About Vidya Bharati University

Discover our rich heritage, guiding principles, and the vision that has shaped generations of leaders, innovators, and change-makers since 1958.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning that transforms lives through quality education, cutting-edge research, and a commitment to service and ethical leadership.

Our Mission

To provide transformative educational experiences that empower students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to address complex global challenges and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our History

Six Decades of Shaping Futures

1958

Foundation

Established by visionary educationist Dr. Ramakrishnan Pillai with a single Engineering college and 200 students.

1975

University Status

Granted autonomous university status by the Government of India, expanding to 8 departments.

1995

Research Excellence

Established the Centre for Advanced Research, marking entry into high-impact scientific research.

2010

International Recognition

Received first QS World University Ranking, acknowledging global research impact.

2024

NAAC A++ Accreditation

Achieved the highest NAAC grade, reaffirming commitment to academic excellence.
